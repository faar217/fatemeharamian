Financial Markets and Instruments (FNCE90047)

This subject introduces domestic and international financial markets, key trading instruments, and financial institutions. Topics covered include financial system structure, money and capital market instruments, financial market dynamics such as volatility and risk, interest rates, inflation, securities regulation, taxation in finance, and various market developments including futures, options, and swaps.

Students can find teaching materials on the University Learning Management System: https://handbook.unimelb.edu.au/2021/subjects/fnce90047
